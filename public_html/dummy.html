Commercially pure Aluminium Alloy 1000 Series Sheets, plates, Pipes, Fittings, Flanges, Extrusions, Round, Hex & Square Bars, Forgings Supplier & Exporter in India & Worldwide.

Aluminium alloy 1000 Series Includes Commercially Pure Aluminium alloy 1050, Aluminium alloy 1060, Aluminium alloy 1100 which is Supplied & Exported in all shapes & Sizes.

Metal Ministry Inc is a leading Stockiest, Supplier & Exporter of 1000 series Aluminium alloys which is purest form of aluminium alloys in the world with a minimum 99% aluminium content which can be supplied in forms of Strips, Sheets, Plates, Round Bars, Hex Bars, Square bars, Aluminium 1000 Series Extrusions & Profiles, Angles & Channels, Seamless & Welded Pipes & Tubes, Forged products, Pipe Fittings, Flanges, Fasteners & Valves as well as 1000 Series aluminium CNC parts as per clients Requirements.

This 1000 series of aluminium alloys are used in Fabrication process to make Pressure vessels & Chemical tanks. 1000 series aluminium alloys are easily weld able and however certain considerations should be made before welding due to the materials narrow melting range. These 1000 series aluminium alloys are widely used in applications including electrical and chemical applications. These commercially pure alloys offer a combination of high corrosion resistance with high thermal and electrical conductivity.

All our 1000 Series Aluminium alloy Grade Products are supplied in Relevant standards as per customer requirements & Mill TC from Indian, USA, South African, Russian, German & European Mills only.
