Hastelloy C-2000 sheets are made with a mix of nickel, chromium, and molybdenum. The Hastelloy C-2000 plates likewise contain an expansion of copper. The expansion of copper helps the Hastelloy UNS N06200 sheets to have upgraded resistivity against sulfuric corrosive. The Chromium content in the Hastelloy C-2000 plates additionally offers more prominent protection from oxidizing synthetics. The Nickel content causes the sheets to have higher malleability and formability. The Hastelloy UNS N06200 plates likewise offer remarkable protection from stress erosion breaking in chloride-bearing arrangements.
